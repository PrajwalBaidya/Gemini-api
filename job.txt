Job Description: AI Engineer

Position Overview

We are seeking a highly motivated AI Engineer to join our team. The
ideal candidate will have a strong background in machine learning, deep
learning, and data science, with the ability to design, build, and
deploy AI-powered applications.

Key Responsibilities

-   Develop, train, and optimize machine learning and deep learning
    models.
-   Research and implement algorithms for natural language processing,
    computer vision, and predictive analytics.
-   Collaborate with cross-functional teams including data engineers,
    software developers, and product managers.
-   Deploy AI solutions into production environments and ensure
    scalability, reliability, and performance.
-   Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI/ML research and
    tools.
-   Conduct experiments, analyze results, and generate insights for
    continuous improvement.

Required Skills & Qualifications

-   Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science,
    AI/ML, or related field.
-   Strong programming skills in Python (TensorFlow, PyTorch,
    scikit-learn, etc.).
-   Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, and statistics.
-   Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) for AI/ML
    deployment.
-   Knowledge of MLOps, model lifecycle management, and version control
    systems (Git).
-   Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Preferred Skills

-   Experience in NLP, computer vision, or reinforcement learning.
-   Familiarity with big data tools (Spark, Hadoop).
-   Contributions to open-source AI/ML projects.
-   Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).

Benefits

-   Competitive salary with performance-based incentives.
-   Health and wellness benefits.
-   Opportunities for continuous learning and professional development.
-   Collaborative and innovative work culture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Location: Flexible (Remote/On-site)
Job Type: Full-time
